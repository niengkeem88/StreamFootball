# Privacy Policy Draft

MatchPulse Live stores local preferences, selected region, terms acceptance, dark mode, refresh settings, and favorite match IDs on the device.

When ads are enabled, Google Mobile Ads may process advertising data according to Google's policies. Optional push notifications and analytics are not active in this v1 build.

Football data is loaded through the configured backend. API-Football keys must remain backend-only.

MatchPulse Live does not host, distribute, or promote unauthorized live streams.
