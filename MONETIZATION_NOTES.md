# Monetization Notes

Google Mobile Ads SDK is integrated with safe defaults.

Allowed placements:

- Home dashboard lower area
- Scores list after match content
- TV Guide after filters
- TV Schedule detail near bottom
- Match Detail near bottom
- Favorites near bottom when content exists

Forbidden placements:

- app launch
- onboarding
- region selection
- terms
- privacy
- settings
- every tab switch
- every match tap
